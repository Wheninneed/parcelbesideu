🚚 Order & Deliver

Already have a Korean address but can't order online? We'll place the order and ship it directly to your door.

5% of item price + $7
Same service, but delivered straight to your Korean address.

How it works

  • Send us what you want. Share a product link or a screenshot of your shopping cart from any Korean or international shopping site.
  • Tell us your address. Provide your Korean home address where you'd like the package delivered.
  • We send you a quote. We'll calculate the total (item + shipping + our service fee) and send you the final price.
  • Pay & we order. Once you pay, we place the order with your address as the shipping destination.
  • Delivered to your door. The package arrives at your home — no office visit needed.

Perfect for you if...

  • You live off-base and have a Korean address
  • You can't use overseas credit cards on Korean sites
  • Language barriers make it hard to navigate Korean shopping malls
  • You want items from AliExpress shipped to Korea (faster than via US)
