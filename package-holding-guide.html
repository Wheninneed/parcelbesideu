📝 How to request a service

Step-by-step guide for using our package holding service.

Step 1. Address

Send your package to our office address.
Please message us via Instagram or Facebook to get the accurate local address before ordering!

Step 2. Notification

Let us know your tracking number and expected delivery date.

Step 3. Pick up

Pick up your package at our office during business hours. The fee is $7 (or 10,000 KRW).
